A Great Public Speaking

in a couple of hour

Introduction

The purpose is to learn how make a great speaking session. Participants in this module will know how focus a speaking in an idea, know the principal tips, good things and bad things during a public speaking and try out to do it with the other participants.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand why is important to be a good presenter
  • Know what could be good or not during a presentation
  • Have actively practiced with a example talk of our self
If this is your first time running a meeting check out the Great Meeting Planning Guide before getting started.

Your Mission: learn the basics of Public Speaking to talk about the Open Source project made by your Club in your University. Are you ready?

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

Get Ready

This activity will help your Club to learn how make a great speaking session! But first you need to get your members excited about make an experience all together about it.

Get people thinking about what is important during a speaking, what is good and what is wrong and complete this two steps:

1) Ask at the group to describe good things in Public Speaking.

Invite them to make a list of these things and put all information in a sticky-notes in a wall or poster-board.

2) Ask at the group to describe bad things in Public Speaking.

Invite them to make a list of these things and put all information in a sticky-notes in another part of the wall or poster-board.

Ask at all the clubber or create little teams of clubber and ask to talk Individually about one Idea, argument, opinion or other topic that all the individuals are in real interested or think is in real important.

A turn, every people or team (5 minutes maximum) must describe the Idea, why is important and why will be important for the others.

Good & Bad example in a presentation (15 Minutes)

Watch all together the video at this link, and Lead a discussion on what people observed.

Discussion:

Explore all things shown by the video and define why every things are a good or a bad thing. For summarize the facilitator could put all good and bad things in sticky-notes.

Get in Action! Final Stage! (5 minutes per each)

Ask at your group to present the same first topic, but, trying to use all the tips and things learned in this workshop.

A turn, every people in the group (5 minutes maximum) must describe the Idea, why is important and why will be important for the others, with all the new things see in the videos and discussed.
